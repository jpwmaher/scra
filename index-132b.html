SCRa - Future concepts


Long term

Summary (dashboard page)

https://scra-prototypes.herokuapp.com/concept-a-patient-dashboard.html
  • This provides a customisable extensible overview of the patient
  • Panels like Alerts & Flags would be mandatory in the view (controlled centrally). They would have rules around what’s included in each of these (e.g. Flags is RA, Chronic conditions, etc)
  • But users would be able to add the panels most relevant to their role to this view
  • All panel content would link through to its relevant page

By category 

https://scra-prototypes.herokuapp.com/concept-a-patient-category.html
  • This information architecture would be defined by card-sorting and tree-jack user testing
  • All addresses could present map view/directions on click
  • All phone numbers would allow direct dialling on mobile devices
  • At the bottom of each page the source of information and date of update would be displayed (this could be done on field level rather than page level)

By category - medication

https://scra-prototypes.herokuapp.com/concept-a-patient-medication.htm
  • This example page shows how content can be enhanced 
    • with medication descriptions and information being available in situ
    • Source of medication to give a full overview (GP, Hosp, Mental, Sexual etc)
    • There are no tables to avoid complications when sizing down to small screens. Each ‘row’ of content neatly stacks into two columns on mobiles.

By category - Mental Health Crisis Plan

https://scra-prototypes.herokuapp.com/concept-a-patient-mhcp.html
  • This Plan would be delivered by NRLS (the source is at the foot of the page), but lives within the defined information hierarchy rather than by its source

By category - results

https://scra-prototypes.herokuapp.com/concept-a-patient-results.html
  • Here we see how results and observations could be brought to life
  • These results would be linked to from both the timeline, and anywhere there is a linked diagnoses / care event / treatment etc

By provider

https://scra-prototypes.herokuapp.com/concept-a-patient-provider.html
https://scra-prototypes.herokuapp.com/concept-a-patient-provider-hospital.html#key-details
  • This will create a section based on all the care providers the patient has a relationship with
  • Structure of each may differ, but certain elements should be consistent, such as
    • contacts, and their details at the top of each provider page
    • condensation timeline view of interactions
    • medications prescribed by that provider
    • admin tasks performed 
  • any media which may help (photos videos)

Timeline

https://scra-prototypes.herokuapp.com/concept-a-patient-timeline.html
  • Research suggests this would be a very popular view
  • It should be searchable and filterable (by type & provider)
  • Each element should link through to relevant page (i.e. heart scan has link to results and diagnoses, care events link to summaries)
