Principles

  1. Patient can be identified explicitly on all pages, on all devices
  2. Patient's flags (FGM, CP-IS, RA) are explicit on all devices
  3. Patient's flags need to explicitly display the status (no flag set, flag set) to those with permission to view them
  4. The portal should surpress content based on viewing permissions, rather than imply meaning via 'greyed out' states
